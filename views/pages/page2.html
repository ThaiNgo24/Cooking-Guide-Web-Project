Chugoko

Chugoku, located in western Japan, is famous for its diverse regional cuisine. Known for its okayama peaches and hiroshima-style okonomiyaki, the region also offers famous oysters and anago (sea eel). The coastal areas are abundant with fresh seafood, while the mountainous regions produce shochu and locally grown rice. Chugoku's food reflects its rich natural environment and long-standing culinary traditions, offering a unique blend of savory and seasonal flavors.
